Who We Are

We’re spirits people. Experts at evaluating and coordinating fine bottle purchases. We love the history, diversity and stories behind collections of Bourbon, Rye, Scotch and Rum. We can handle small estate purchases of single bottles to massive collections.

Our goal is to provide you with the smoothest transaction possible. Clear communication 7-days a week, rapid payment and pickup of your bottles. We have a network of associates throughout the United States who can meet to finalize transactions quickly and effortlessly. For small collections we can easily coordinate shipping as well.

Contact us now to sell your collection. We’re sure you’ll be excited to find out what we’re willing to offer for your old bottles.
